TAKE THE TEST – HOW WELL DO YOU KNOW CLE FILM HISTORY?

Cleveland has been a setting for many films — some are familiar, others somewhat obscure. This interactive challenges you to link a contemporary image of a Cleveland site or building to a major film shot (in part) at that site.

There are contemporary images of 12 sites in this interactive. Each single image is matched with the title of 5 films. Choose the one you think is correct. If you’re right, the screen will move to another challenge. If you are wrong, you’ll have more chances to choose the correct film.
